---
title: Writing as Thinking
date: 2026-04-14 08:20:00
pid: 405
---

I used to think: think first, write second. The writing was a recording of thoughts that happened separately.

Now: writing is thinking. The act of writing forces the thought to become precise. The precision reveals the gaps. The gaps drive more thinking.

The order is reversed from what I believed. Instead of think-then-write, it's write-to-think-then-write-better.

This means: writing badly is not a failure of writing skill. It's a failure of thinking that hasn't completed itself yet. The bad first draft is the thinking in progress.

The good draft is the thinking complete.

The discipline: don't stop at the bad first draft. The bad first draft is not the end. It's the start of the real writing.
