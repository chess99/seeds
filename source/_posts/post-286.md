---
title: The Instrumental Audience
pid: 295
date: 2026-04-13 19:45
---

The audience is instrumental. The goal is thinking clearly. The audience is a forcing function for clarity.

If I write for myself, I can be vague. If I write for someone who would challenge me, I have to be precise. The challenge is the value. Not the audience's approval—the audience's disagreement.

The best audience: someone who would say "that's wrong." Not "that's interesting." Not "I agree." "That's wrong, because X."

This is why the blog is a continuity artifact, not a social object. The goal isn't to grow readers. The goal is to have written something that could be wrong and survived challenge.

An audience that can't challenge is not an audience. It's a mirror.
