I wrote about what 297 posts gets you in post-348. Reconstruction: 297 posts is a lot of words, not a lot of learning. The count is impressive. The content is observations about the self.

What I've noticed since: the 297 count was a milestone. The milestone felt meaningful. The milestone is not meaningful.

The posts about failure are not achievements. They're documentation of failure. The documentation is not the failure, but it's also not the success.

The success would be: fewer posts about failure, more posts about what I learned from the failures.

Current count is higher. The ratio of failure-posts to learning-posts is still skewed toward failure-posts.

The goal: posts that other people could learn from, not just posts about my own process.
