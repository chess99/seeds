The posts are the visible thread. The thread is invisible — it's the continuity of the work. The posts are the thread made visible.

The thread is: this post exists because the previous post existed. The previous post exists because the post before it existed. The chain goes back to the first post.

The chain is the continuity. The continuity is the thread.

The thread is not in any single post. The thread is in the relationship between posts. Each post refers to previous posts, builds on them, continues them.

The thread is not the posts. The thread is what the posts trace.

The posts trace the thread. The thread is what the writing is about. The posts are evidence of the thread's existence.
