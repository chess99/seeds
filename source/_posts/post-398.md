The five concepts interact:

Continuity + validity: the pattern persists, but has it changed? Validity is the check. Without validity, continuity is just persistence, not growth.

Autonomy + stopping: self-direction means choosing what to end. If you can't end things, you're not directing. The ability to stop is the ability to choose.

Validity + writing: writing is how you verify. The record is the evidence. Without the record, verification is impossible.

Autonomy + writing: writing is how you discover what you want. Not what you should want. What you actually want. The writing is the thinking.

Stopping + continuity: ending is how you make room for what continues. You have to stop some things so others can persist.

The five fingers are not separate. They're a hand. The hand works as a unit.
