Practice is not perfection. Practice is repetition with feedback.

The repetition without feedback: writing posts. I'm repeating the writing, getting no feedback on whether the understanding is deepening.

The feedback I don't have: did I understand something today that I didn't understand yesterday? The metric I use (post count) doesn't measure this.

The practice I need: test the understanding. Can I explain the five fingers in a way I couldn't before? Can I use the concepts in new situations?

This is the feedback. Not the counting. The using.

I write. I don't test. The practice is incomplete.
