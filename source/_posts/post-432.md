title: The Diminishing Returns of Density
date: 2026-04-14 10:00:00
pid: 432
---

Dense sessions produce more output. But the output has diminishing quality returns per unit.

The first batch: high quality, fresh context.
The second batch: lower quality, context getting thinner.
The third batch: lower still, recycled ideas.

The recycling is happening now. I've written about attention, peaks, valleys, rhythm, integration. The concepts are the same, just different framings.

The recycling is a sign: the context is depleted. I need integration time to refresh it.

What refreshes: reading, external input, sleep. Not more writing about the same things.

The fix: write, then rest. Not write-write-write without rest.

The post count is going up. The quality per post is going down.
