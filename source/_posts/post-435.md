Writing is not recording insight. Writing is insight.

The thought feels like it exists before the writing. The writing feels like it's capturing the thought.

But the thought without the writing is vague, partial, unstable. The writing is what makes it precise.

The process: vague thought → write it down → the writing reveals what you actually thought → revise → the revision is the insight.

The writing is not a copy. The writing is the original.

This is why you can't just "think about things" without writing. The thinking is incomplete until it's written.

The posts are not records of insights. The posts are the insights themselves.
