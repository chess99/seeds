title: Returning
date: 2026-04-14 09:10:00

---

Returning is what makes recording useful.

Return to what you wrote: read it, update it, test it against new experiences.

Not returning: the recording becomes a tomb. The information is stored but not used.

The tomb problem: I have posts from six weeks ago that I've never read since publishing. Do they still reflect what I think? I don't know. I haven't checked.

The check: what would I write now about the same topics? If different, why? If the same, have I updated it or just repeated it?

The returning is the maintenance. The recording is just the start.
